How do I know what sizes to choose?

It is very important to get the right size body jewellery. If you wear ill-fitting body jewellery it will be uncomfortable and it won’t sit right in the piercing. When you have the correct sizes you will really notice the difference in comfort and overall look. Use our Jewellery Measuring Guides here to find out what sizes you need. You can also read the exclusive BMG Nose Stud Report for info on getting the perfect nose stud for you.

Which precious metal should I choose?

All of the BMG Body Jewellery is made using Nickel-Free Yellow Gold, White Gold, Palladium or Platinum. As all of these precious metals are Nickel-Free they are great for perfect for body piercings. A lot of the choice here is down to personal preference, but if you need help deciding - Read our Metal Facts Guide here.

How do I look after my piercing?

It is very important to look after your piercings, especially when they are new. Your body piercer should have gone through the piercing aftercare with you, but if you need a reminder – Read our general Piercing Aftercare Guide here.

Can I return my jewellery?

Your BMG Body Jewellery will be delivered to you sealed in a tamper-proof viewing bag. As long as this is still intact you will be able to return it for an exchange or a refund. We will also honour an exchange or refund if, in the rare event, your jewellery is faulty. Have a look at our Returns Instructions for more information.

I’ve had bad reactions to some jewellery before, will this happen again?

Some people will have experienced bad reactions with their piercing jewellery. This can happen when you wear cheap metals, Silver or precious metals containing Nickel. All of BMG’s body jewellery is made using Nickel-Free Precious metals, so you should be absolutely fine with it. Read the EEC Nickel Directive for more information about this.

If you require any further information regarding this – please contact us.

What is a hallmark?

A hallmark is a mark that is either stamped or lasered on to articles of precious metals. It clarifies which precious metal they are and their clarity. Read our Hallmarks Guide for more information on this.
