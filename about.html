About Us

Our company was founded in 1998 and together with our 7 members of staff we have been adorning you and your loved ones with the World's Finest Body Jewellery. We are one of the only companies in the world that understand jewellery, piercing, and piercing jewellery - how it works & how it needs to fit. Our goldsmiths are highly skilled craftsmen and are proud to work for you.

BMG have the largest collection of specialised Diamond Nose Studs - which lie flat against the nose - giving YOU the perfect look! We also have an amazing collection of designer Belly Bars, BCRs, Barbells, Labret Studs, Ear Jewellery and many many more! Our collections are ever expanding, so be sure to take a look regularly. Also check out our Gold Nipple Rings and Bars, PA Rings, Clit Rings and other Genital Jewelry. PLUS all of our body jewellery is nickel free. So you don't need to worry about having bad reactions to cheap metals.

We take special pride in offering you the finest and most fashionable piercing jewellery available. Browse the beautiful selections of unique body jewellery and remember we can custom make or design anything just for you! Our diamond belly bars and nose studs are second to none, along with some stunning nipple rings, nipple bars and belly ring designs. If you can't find what you're looking for - Why not ask us to design you a piece? We will work closely with you to help bring your jewellery ideas to life! Contact us for more information on this bespoke service. Take a look at our Custom Galleries to see more.

Here at BMG have our own expert team including in house designers and goldsmiths. So we can guarantee your new jewellery will be hand-made in store with us, and shipped straight out to you. This means we can also offer you a fantastic alterations service. So if your new jewellery is too big - simply just send it back and we can shorten it for you. Click here for more details on our alterations service. We also take great care to keep our customer services at its best. Take a look at our body jewellery testimonials to see what our customers have to say about us.

We are based in the UK, but ship to most places in the world. Please visit the Delivery section to see more delivery options. We also give you an unbelievable offer of a 3 year guarantee as well as a certificate of authenticity and gift box with each and every purchase!

Please remember – We are always open to your requests and we'd love to hear from you! Simply contact us at all@bodymattersgold.com or call us on 01444 871369 or +44 1444 4871369 from outside UK.
